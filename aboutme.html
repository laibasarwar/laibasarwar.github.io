About Me

I am currently a sophomore at New York University Tandon School of Engineering and enrolled in the Integrated Digital Media (IDM) program. At first, I applied for the Civil Engineering program, but after going to numerous career panels, and attending a Computational Photography elective, I decided to follow my actual interests, and change to IDM. 

As for this portfolio, I wanted to showcase my interests and the work that I have done so far. Since I was born in Pakistan, and raised in New York from the age of four, I wanted to showcase the split identity that I had through my projects. With each project, there is a hint of a home that was never really there, and you can hopefully see all of this while exploring the site. Thank you!
